Privacy Policy

Pukeko AR Experience
Effective Date: August 28, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the Pukeko AR Experience (“we,” “our,” “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our web application, including the interactive Q&A feature and Selfie Mode powered by the Snap Camera Kit Web SDK.

By using the Pukeko AR Experience, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Scope

This policy applies to the Pukeko AR Experience web application and any related pages where it is posted or linked.

Information We Collect

  • Camera Access (Real‑Time Only): To enable AR functionality, the app requires access to your device’s camera. Camera input is processed locally in real time via Snap Camera Kit to render AR effects. Camera data is not stored, recorded, or transmitted to our servers.
  • Photos You Capture: When you take a selfie with the AR Pukeko, the image is generated and stored locally on your device. We do not automatically upload, store, or share your photos. You decide whether to save or share them.
  • Interactive Q&A Text: Text you enter for the Q&A feature is processed client‑side to generate a response. We do not collect, log, or transmit this text to our servers.
  • Analytics / Tracking: We do not use analytics, trackers, or cookies for profiling or marketing. We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or precise location.

How We Use Information

  • To provide AR functionality through Snap Camera Kit.
  • To deliver interactive Q&A responses client‑side.
  • To operate and maintain the application.

We do not sell personal information and we do not use information for targeted advertising.

Processing & Retention

  • Local Processing: Camera data and Q&A inputs are processed locally on your device. We do not maintain server‑side logs of your interactions.
  • User‑Initiated Storage Only: Photos are stored only if you choose to save them on your device.
  • No Biometric Templates: We do not create or store face templates or other biometric identifiers.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We may share limited information only:

  • Service Enablement: With Snap Inc. services solely to enable AR effects via Snap Camera Kit.
  • Legal: If required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the safety of users and the integrity of our service.

Children’s Privacy

The Pukeko AR Experience is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, or to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

Third‑Party Services

The app uses the Snap Camera Kit Web SDK to enable AR effects. Snap Inc. may process certain data as part of this functionality. For more information, please review Snap’s Privacy Policy: https://values.snap.com/privacy/privacy-policy.
